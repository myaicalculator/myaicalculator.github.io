Steps to Install Solar

1

Site Assessment & Design

Professional assessment of your roof, shading analysis, and custom system design based on your energy needs and roof specifications.

2

Permits & Approvals

Obtain necessary permits from local authorities and grid connection approval from your electricity distribution company (DISCOM).

3

Installation & Setup

Professional installation of solar panels, inverters, and electrical components. Typically completed in 1-3 days depending on system size.

4

Grid Connection & Commissioning

Grid synchronization, net meter installation, and system commissioning with your local DISCOM for net metering benefits.

5

Monitoring & Maintenance

Regular monitoring of system performance and minimal maintenance including periodic cleaning and annual inspections.

Net metering allows you to sell excess solar power back to the grid and get credits on your electricity bill. During daytime, excess solar power is fed into the grid, and you draw power during nights/cloudy days. Most Indian states have net metering policies, though settlement rates and regulations vary by DISCOM.
